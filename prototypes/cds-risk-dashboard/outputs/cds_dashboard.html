Explanation Caveats

The display shows which synthetic signals are present near the score. It does not prove causality, explain a real patient trajectory, or validate any action.

The uncertainty band widens when synthetic inputs are missing or the acuity signal is escalating.

Limitations Visible With Output

Risk values describe generated labels in a simulated dataset. They should not be used for triage, diagnosis, treatment, staffing, or real workflow decisions.

Workflow claims from this page are design hypotheses only.
