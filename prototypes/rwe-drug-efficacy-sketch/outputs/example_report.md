- Treatment assignment depends on age, comorbidity score, prior event, and frailty index.

## Limitations

- All estimates are associations unless an explicit causal argument is made outside this report.
- The propensity model is intentionally small and fit only to observed synthetic covariates.
- Good behavior on this generator does not imply good behavior on real healthcare data.
